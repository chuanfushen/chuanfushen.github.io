Research

My primary research interests lie in human retrieval, gait recognition, and 3D perception. Some papers are highlighted.

LidarGait: Benchmarking 3D Gait Recognition with Point Clouds
Chuanfu Shen, Chao Fan, Wei Wu, Rui Wang, George Q. Huang, Shiqi Yu
CVPR, 2023
project page / arXiv / Dataset

A benchmark and baseline model for learning gait representations from 3D point clouds. Leveraging the proposed SUSTech1K dataset with 1,050 IDs, it enables the extraction of gait features by projecting 3D geometric points onto a range-view representation.
